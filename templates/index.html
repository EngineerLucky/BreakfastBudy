About BreakfastBuddy

BreakfastBuddy was inspired by the need to streamline canteen management and improve the overall dining experience. Our journey began with a vision to create a user-friendly platform that simplifies ordering and payment processes. This project is part of our portfolio for Holberton School, demonstrating our commitment to developing practical solutions with real-world applications.
