About BreakfastBuddy

Inspired by the need to streamline canteen management and provide a seamless experience for both staff and customers, BreakfastBuddy was created to offer an all-in-one solution. Our journey began as a portfolio project for Holberton School, and it has grown into a comprehensive tool designed to make canteen operations more efficient and enjoyable.
